About Our Habit Tracker

Welcome to the Monthly Habit Tracker! This application helps you build and maintain positive habits by tracking your daily progress in a simple, visual way.

Features:

  • Track multiple habits per month
  • Visual calendar view for easy progress tracking
  • Add and remove custom habits
  • Data saved securely in MongoDB Atlas
  • User authentication for personalized tracking
  • Responsive design for mobile and desktop

How to Use:

  1. Sign up or log in to your account
  2. Add habits you want to track
  3. Click on calendar cells to mark habits as done (✅), missed (❌), or not marked
  4. Navigate between months to view your progress over time
